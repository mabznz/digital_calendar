Racing Calendar

With the TAB Digital Racing Calendar, you can select and add race meetings to the calendar on your personal device. Then set reminders to get your bet on, watch an event on Trackside or attend one of the many on-course events throughout NZ.

Subscribe with iCalendar

You can add our digital calendar on your digital devices (Android, iPhone or other calendars that allow importing an '.ics' file) Once downloaded, all race meetings will be scheduled in your calendar. And you can add a reminder to notify you before each race meeting starts.
